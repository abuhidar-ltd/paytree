# Paytree Marketing Playbook

## Target Personas

### PERSONA 1 — The Trader (Primary Target)
Age: 22-35, Male
Platforms: Twitter/X, TikTok, YouTube
Pain points:
  - Linktree takes 9% of every course sale
  - No way to create FOMO for launches
  - Analytics don't show where audience comes from
  - Bio link looks cheap and generic
Dream outcome:
  - Passive income while sleeping
  - Page that looks as premium as their brand
  - AI handling sales questions 24/7
Hook: "You made $5,000 this month. Linktree took $450."

### PERSONA 2 — The Educator
Age: 25-40
Platforms: YouTube, Instagram, newsletters
Pain points:
  - Can't gate free resources for email capture
  - No way to build email list from bio link
  - Course sales require multiple tools
Dream outcome:
  - Every free resource builds email list
  - One link handles courses, community, content
Hook: "Turn every free PDF into 100 email subscribers"

### PERSONA 3 — The Coach
Age: 28-45
Platforms: Instagram, LinkedIn
Pain points:
  - Bio link looks unprofessional
  - Booking and payment are separate tools
  - No way to showcase results/stats
Dream outcome:
  - One premium link that does everything
  - Automated lead capture
Hook: "Your entire coaching business. One link."
